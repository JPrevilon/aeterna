# Medical and Wellness Copy Rules

Aeterna includes medical-adjacent services: GLP-1 weight loss, hormone balance, IV therapy, vitamin injections, Emsculpt, Emtone, Emsella, and Exilis Ultra. Treat all public language as consult-first and provider-guided.

## Safe language

Use:

```txt
designed to support
may help support
consultation required
provider-guided
when clinically appropriate
eligibility will be reviewed
results vary
not a substitute for emergency care
contraindications may apply
```

## Avoid

Do not use:

```txt
guaranteed results
cures
reverses disease
prevents stroke
no risk
permanent results
FDA approved for every claim
lose X pounds guaranteed
```

## Suggested disclaimer

```txt
Aeterna services are designed for wellness, beauty, and personalized care. Medical wellness services require consultation and provider review. Treatment eligibility, protocols, and results vary by client. Information on this website is not medical advice and does not replace care from a licensed medical provider.
```

## Where to show disclaimers

- Medical Wellness page
- GLP-1 service page
- Hormone Balance service page
- IV Therapy page
- Body Sculpting pages
- Booking confirmation
- Policies page
