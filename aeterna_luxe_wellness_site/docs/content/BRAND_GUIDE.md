# Aeterna Brand Guide

## Positioning

Aeterna is a luxury wellness sanctuary blending spa rituals, beauty treatments, medical wellness, longevity care, body sculpting, hormone balance, IV therapy, and personalized weight-loss support.

## Tone

- Calm
- Refined
- Confident
- Clinical where necessary
- Never loud or gimmicky
- Luxury hospitality first, with clear medical-spa credibility

## Fonts

Recommended production pair:

```txt
Headings: Cormorant Garamond or Playfair Display
Body: Inter, Manrope, or Neue Haas-style sans serif
```

Current site uses Google-hosted Cormorant Garamond + Manrope through Next.js font optimization.

## Logo usage

Use:

```txt
aeterna-full-logo.png — footer, about, PDFs, social previews
aeterna-wordmark-logo.png — header, hero, gift card section
aeterna-symbol.png — favicon-style usage, loading screen, watermark, floating animation
```

## Photography direction

Use warm cream interiors, candlelight, plants, linens, soft shelves, earthy brown tones, and refined editorial crops. Emerald should be an accent, not the dominant field color. Treatment navigation should pair a clear label with a relevant image wherever possible.

## Medical trust cues

Use blue shields, stethoscopes, Rx marks, and screening icons sparingly to distinguish clinical information from spa content. Do not display FDA approval, HIPAA, LegitScript, pharmacy, provider-credential, or other third-party certification claims without current documentation that specifically applies to Aeterna and the service being described.

## Copy examples

```txt
Enter a sanctuary for beauty, balance, longevity, and renewal.

Wellness treated as a ritual. Beauty treated as a long-term relationship.

Begin with a private consultation and leave with a personalized plan.
```

## Avoid

- Loud neon medical spa visuals
- Overpromising medical outcomes
- Harsh before/after marketing on homepage
- Claims like “guaranteed,” “cures,” “reverses disease,” or “no risk”
